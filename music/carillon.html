About me and the carillon

I started to play the Hopeman Memorial Carillon at University of Rochester since my junior year (September 2018). I joined the Carillon class MUR104 and practiced under our instructor, Ms. Doris Aman. Through my two years of experience, I arranged and performed different pieces for various occasions such as Halloween, Earth Hour or carillon exhibition.

Following are the musics I've played at my time at the University of Rochester, and a concert recording on 02-22-2020.
